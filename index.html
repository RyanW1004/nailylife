Bio

Hi I'm Becka and I'm a nail art addict.
I've been doing my nails for as long as I can remember, and I love coming up with new takes on trending designs.
My all time favourite designs are anything sparkly, glittery, bright, or floral! All of the above form a large part of my 'nail-fies' so expect to see more of them!
I've started this site to showcase my designs in a format which is easier to track than Instagram.
We are also prototyping a 'new home finder' for the nail varnish that you no longer use or want! Hopefully that functionality will be up and running soon but in the mean time please feel free to browse and enjoy the content I put here!
Thanks again for visiting hope to see you again soon!
